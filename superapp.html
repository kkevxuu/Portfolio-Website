Why

Furniture often fades into the background of everyday life, contrained by the burdens of practicality. I wanted to attempt to design a chair that brings the grace and beauty of dance into everyday life.

What

A lounge chair insired by the Chaoxian (Korean) traditional crane dance, created through deriving the form, flow, and movement of dancers from snapshots of their choreohraphy.

How

Rhino 3D, painted wood, rope.
