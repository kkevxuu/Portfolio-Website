Crane Chairs

Why

Furniture often fades into the background of everyday life, contrained by the burdens of practicality. I wanted to attempt to design a chair that brings the grace and beauty of dance into everyday life.

What

A lounge chair insired by the Josenjok (Korean) crane dance, created through deriving the form, flow, and movement of dancers from snapshots of their choreohraphy.

How

Rhino 3D, painted wood, rope.

Can a chair be designed to bring the beauty of dance into eyeryday life?

The Crane dance is a traditional folk dance of the Korean ethnic group in China. It originated as imitations of the elegant movements of cranes, and dancers wear flowing clothing that enhances their movements, creating a sense of grace and dynamism in motion.

Physical prototype model

Visual lightness was a central theme when considering combination of materials.

Informed by tradition

Inspired by looms used in the traditional textiles and tapestry, the rope weave construction pays tribute to the clothing worn by the dancers.

Iterations

Three ideas were explored before the process of designing the final chair, each were inspired by different movements within the dance.
