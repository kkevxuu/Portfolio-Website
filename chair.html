Form is Function

Minimalism has been a rising trend in design. Many great designs we know today helped popularize the philosophy of minimalism and function driven form, stripping down everything to its essentials. However, many designs have started to look similar and uninspiring in pursuit of minimalism. Products that blindly follow minimalism frequently sacrifice the emotional component of design.
